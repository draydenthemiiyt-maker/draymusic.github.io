About Chris
Chris R. Lillo is a rock and heavy metal artist from Chile, known for his energetic style and ability to blend powerful melodies with deep lyrics. Immersed in the music scene since the age of 13, Chris draws inspiration from rock classics and contemporary metal icons, creating a unique sound that captivates fans of both genres.

Chris's dedication to authenticity and artistic expression is reflected in every note he plays and every word he sings.
